3.3.2 Mutation Analysis of the BTK gene Institute of Life Sciences

Institute for Life Sciences (HU)

Utrecht, The Netherlands

2018

  • The immune deficiency X‑linked agammaglobulinemia (XLA) is caused by mutations in the beta tyrosine kinase (BTK) gene. The weakened immune system is directly related to faulty B‑cell formation.
  • DNA was isolated and amplified using PCR from XLA patients and healthy patients.
  • Custom primers were created to target the BTK gene, which was then sequenced. The location of mutations was discovered using the software DNA baser.
